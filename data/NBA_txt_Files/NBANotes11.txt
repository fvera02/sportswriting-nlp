SPURS-THUNDER 2018-19 SERIES RECAP:The Spurs hold a 1-0 series lead over the Thunder in 2018-19 ... In a record-setting, double- overtime affair the Spurs prevailed over the Thunder on 1/10 at the AT&T Center. The Spurs set a new NBA record by shooting 84.2 percent from behind the arc (10+ makes), converting on their first 14 three-point attempts before recording a miss from behind the arc. LaMarcus Aldridge poured in 56 points (20-33 FG, 16-16 FT) to set a new career-best performance, adding 9 rebounds, 4 assists and 4 blocked shots. Derrick White also notched a new career high scoring night, racking up 23 points (10-17 FG, 1-3 3FG, 2-2 FT), recording 8 assists, five boards, 2 steals and 1 block. On the other side, Russell Westbrook set a new NBA season high with his 24 points to complement 24 points and 13 boards to secure a triple-double. Paul George added 30 points (11-25 FG, 3-7 3FG, 5-7 FT). Oklahoma City trailed by as many as 16 before climbing back to push the game into overtime ... The Spurs and Thunder met for the second time in three nights, with Oklahoma City bouncing back from its double-overtime loss to record a 122-112 win at Chesapeake Energy Arena on 1/12. Marco Belinelli scored a season-high 24 points (8-10 FG, 4-6 3FG, 4-4 FT), providing the Spurs with a spark off the bench. San Antonio shot 10-of-31 (.323) from distance a game after shooting an NBA-record 84.2 percent. Russell Westbrook paced Oklahoma City with 24 points (9- 24 FG, 1-2 3FG, 5-5 FT), 10 rebounds, 7 assists and 2 steals.
