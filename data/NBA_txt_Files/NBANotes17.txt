Brandon Ingram and LeBron James each scored 31 points, but the Lakers fell to the Bucks in their first meeting of the season in a game that saw 20 ties and 17 lead chang- es. Milwaukee closed the game on a 15-2 run to steal the win in Los Angeles. James tallied his 26th double-double of the season with 31 points, 10 assists and 7 rebounds, while Ingram continued his stellar play with 31 points, 8 rebounds and 2 assists. Rajon Rondo recorded his first 20-point game of the season along with 8 rebounds and 9 assists. Kyle Kuzma also scored in double figures for the Lakers with 12 points and 6 rebounds. Eric Bledsoe led the Bucks with 31 points, 9 rebounds and 5 assists, while Giannis Antetokounmpo finished with 16 points, 15 rebounds, 6 assists and 3 blocks. Malcolm Brogdon (21 points), Khris Middleton (18 points), Ersan Ilyasova (12 points), Brook Lopez (11 points) and Nikola Mirotic (11 points) all scored in double-figures for the Bucks. The two teams have met 218 times overall and on 152 occasions since the Clippers moved to Los Angeles ...
